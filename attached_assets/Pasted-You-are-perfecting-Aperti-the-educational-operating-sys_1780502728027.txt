## 2. COURSE MANAGEMENT & TEACHER WORKFLOW

- **Subject Linking:** When a teacher creates a course, they must select an existing subject from the global list (created by admin). They cannot add new global subjects.
- **Lesson Delivery Type:** Teacher chooses: Online, Centre‑based, or Both/Hybrid.
- **Subscription & Payment Settings:**  
  - Teacher sets course fees and payment methods. Options:
    - Per lesson
    - Monthly
    - Full course (one‑time)
    - Half‑course installments (teacher specifies number of installments, deadlines, and amount per installment in EGP).
  - The system must support multiple payment methods for a single course (parents choose at checkout).
- **Package Inclusions:** Teacher toggles:
  - Whether lessons are recorded and included in the package.
  - Whether material fees are included, or if they are separate (and the exact cost).
- **Flexible Payment Options:** Parents/students can choose from the available payment methods when enrolling.

## 4. TEACHER VERIFICATION & EVALUATIONS

- **Verified Badge:**  
  - After a teacher reaches 100+ enrolled students, they become eligible for a “Verified” badge.  
  - The admin must manually approve the badge from the admin panel.  
  - The badge is displayed on the teacher’s public profile and course cards.
- **Weekly Teacher Evaluations:**  
  - When a student is enrolled with a teacher, both the student and their parent receive a prompt every week to evaluate the teacher (course duration dependent).  
  - The teacher, during course creation, must define the evaluation prompts/questions.
  - The evaluation data is stored for future AI analysis.
- **System‑Wide Website Evaluation:**  
  - Every 2 weeks, all users see a short, clean form asking them to rate the website and suggest improvements.  
  - Admin can view aggregated results.
