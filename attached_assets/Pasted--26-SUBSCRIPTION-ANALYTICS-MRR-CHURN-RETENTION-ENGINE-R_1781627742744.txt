Build a Payment Recovery System that handles failed or incomplete payments automatically.


---

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Recover revenue without user friction.


---

🧠 RECOVERY STATES

pending_payment

pending_confirmation

failed_payment

retry_scheduled

recovered

permanently_failed



---

🔁 RECOVERY LOGIC

If payment fails:

1. do NOT cancel subscription immediately


2. move to retry_scheduled


3. allow limited grace access


4. retry Instapay confirmation window


5. if success → restore subscription automatically




---

⏳ RULES

max retry attempts = 3

retry window = configurable (default 48h)

after failure → move to grace_period



---

📊 OUTPUT

  transaction_id: string,
  status: string,
  attempts: number,
  next_retry_at: string,
  resolution: "recovered" | "failed"
}
