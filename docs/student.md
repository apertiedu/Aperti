# Student Guide — Aperti

## Getting Started
Sign in at `/login` with the credentials your teacher provided. You'll land on your **Student Dashboard**.

## Key Features

### Study Stream
Your personalised feed of upcoming lessons, due assignments, and AI study tips.
Dashboard → Study Stream.

### Flashcards
Study → Flashcards → pick a deck → tap to flip cards → rate each card (Easy / Okay / Difficult).
Cards you find difficult appear more often using spaced repetition.

### Assessments
Assessments tab → click an assessment → Start.
Your results and feedback appear immediately after submission.

### Revision Notes
Study → Revision Notes → pick a subject → read your teacher's notes.
You can highlight and annotate your own notes.

### Exam Readiness
Study → Exam Readiness → see your predicted grade and recommended topics to revise.

### Practice Exams
Study → Practice → select a past paper → attempt under timed conditions.

## FAQs

**Q: I can't see my teacher's assessment.**
A: Make sure you are enrolled in their course. Contact your teacher if enrollment is pending.

**Q: My score seems wrong.**
A: Check the marking scheme on the assessment detail page. Raise a query via the Discuss button.

**Q: How do I track my progress?**
A: Dashboard → Progress → see your marks, attendance, and topic mastery over time.
