# Student Guide — Aperti

## Getting Started
Sign in at `/login` with the credentials your teacher provided. You'll land on your **Student Dashboard**.

## Key Features

### Study Stream
Your personalised feed of upcoming lessons, due assignments, and AI study tips.
Dashboard → Study Stream.

### Flashcards (My Cardstack)
Study → My Flashcards → pick a deck → tap to flip cards.
After flipping, rate each card:
- **Easy** — you recalled it instantly (card appears less frequently)
- **Okay** — you needed some thought
- **Hard** — you struggled (card reappears very soon)

The platform uses **SM-2 spaced repetition** — hard cards come back sooner, mastered cards appear less. At the end of each session, see your mastery score and session breakdown.

### Assessments
Assessments tab → click an assessment → Start.
Your results and feedback appear immediately after submission.

### Revision Notes
Study → Revision Notes → pick a subject → read your teacher's notes.
You can highlight and annotate your own notes.

### Exam Readiness
Study → Exam Readiness → see your predicted grade and recommended topics to revise.

### Practice Exams
Study → Practice → select a past paper → attempt under timed conditions.

### AI Mentor (The Mentor)
Study → The Mentor → ask any question in natural language.
Supports follow-up questions, worked examples, and step-by-step explanations.

### Search
Use the search bar at the top to find:
- Courses, flashcard decks, revision notes, questions
- Syllabus codes: type "0625" to find all Physics resources
- Weak topics: type "show my struggling topics"

## FAQs

**Why does a hard card keep coming back?**
Spaced repetition means difficult cards repeat more often so you learn them deeply. Rate it Easy once you know it well.

**Can I see my grade before an assessment closes?**
No — grades are released by your teacher after submission.

**How do I link my parent's account?**
Ask your teacher for your student code. Your parent enters it at `/parent/link-student`.

**What is Exam Readiness score?**
A predicted performance score based on your assessment history and topic mastery, updated weekly.
