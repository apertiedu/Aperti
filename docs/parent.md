# Parent Guide — Aperti

## Getting Started
Sign in at `/login`. You'll see a real-time snapshot of your child's academic activity.

## Key Features

### Parent Dashboard
Overview of attendance, recent marks, upcoming assessments, and AI insights for each child.

### Switching Between Children
Use the **child switcher** at the top of the page if you have more than one child enrolled.

### Viewing Marks & Progress
Dashboard → Marks — see individual assessment scores and overall grade trends.

### Attendance
Dashboard → Attendance — view your child's attendance record by subject and date.

### Communication
Dashboard → Messages — send a message to your child's teacher.

### Notifications
Enable push notifications (tap the bell / Enable button) to receive alerts for new marks, attendance issues, and teacher announcements.

## FAQs

**Q: I can't see my child's data.**
A: Your account must be linked to your child by the school admin. Contact the admin to link accounts.

**Q: My child's grade looks wrong.**
A: View the assessment detail for the breakdown. If something is incorrect, use Messages to contact the teacher.
